Research / Projects

MARC: Meta-Cognitively Adaptive Risk Control for 360° ABR

Risk-aware audiovisual ABR
  • Engineered a risk-aware adaptive bitrate (ABR) controller that uses Unscented Kalman Filter (UKF) entropy to minimize poor QoE events (e.g., rebuffering) in volatile networks.
  • Implemented an uncertainty-gated fusion mechanism that intelligently combines video and spatial audio saliency data to make more robust bitrate decisions.
  • Designed to deliver an A% improvement in 1st-percentile QoE and increase bandwidth efficiency by B%, with a decision latency under 50 ms.

Unsupervised Bayesian Surprise Detection in Spatial Audio (Published)

IMX Workshops ’24
  • Architected an unsupervised CVAE-LSTM pipeline to detect Bayesian surprise in real-time spatial audio streams (FOA B-format), successfully modeling the Temporal Evolution of Attention (TEA).
  • Processed audio signals through a custom STFT, mel-spectrogram, and Audio Energy Map (AEM) pipeline to extract and localize salient audio events for the model.
  • Achieved a high F1-score on a key test scenario by defining a novel surprise metric combining reconstruction loss (NLL) and predictive latent-space divergence (KL).
