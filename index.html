Projects & Research

SAE — Spatiotemporal Attentive Entropy

Viewport Prediction

SO(3)-invariant cross-entropy on S² between vMF attention and prediction; O(1) streaming estimator; Fisher-weighted multiscale fusion, integrated with a lightweight LSTM for realtime forecasting.

  • Established accuracy–latency Pareto frontier with realtime E2E latency budgets (CPU/GPU prototypes).
  • Validated calibration (ECE-sphere, NLL); robust to sampling-rate shifts / synthetic dropouts.
  • Artifacts planned: deterministic splits, Docker/LUTs.
Repo (incoming)

MARC — Meta-Cognitively Adaptive Risk Control

Risk-Aware ABR

Couples UKF posterior entropy to a CVaR penalty to hedge tail-QoE under volatile networks; fuses video + spatial audio in the control loop.

  • Vectorized coarse-to-fine candidate search; entropy-gated saliency; optional per-tile risk micro-actuation.
  • Engineering focus: decision latency ≤ realtime budget; throughput/quality balance.
Repo (incoming)

Bayesian Surprise Detection for Spatial Audio

Published — IMX ’24

Unsupervised CVAE–LSTM on FOA (4-ch B-format) modeling TEA (Temporal Evolution of Attention). Pipeline: 16 kHz → STFT + pre-emphasis + Hamming → log mel-spectrogram; AEM via circular harmonics to localize sources.

  • Surprise metric: weighted negative expected log-likelihood + KL between LSTM-predicted next-latent and CVAE posterior.
  • Achieved F1=100% on studied scenario; artifacts planned.
DOI: 10.1145/3672406.3672422
