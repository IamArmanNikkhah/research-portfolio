Selected Projects

MARC — Risk-Aware ABR for 360° (Video + Spatial Audio)

Realtime Control

UKF posterior entropy coupled to CVaR to hedge tail-QoE; fuses video and spatial audio signal for holistic QoE control.

  • Vectorized coarse-to-fine candidate selection; entropy-gated saliency; optional per-tile risk micro-actuation.
  • Confirm & Fill: +A% 1st-pct QoE, +B% bandwidth efficiency; ≤C ms decision latency.

SAE — Spatiotemporal Attentive Entropy on S²

Viewport Prediction

Geometry-correct cross-entropy between vMF attention and prediction; SO(3)-invariant; lightweight LSTM forecaster for realtime.

  • ECE-sphere/NLL calibration; robust to sampling-rate shifts & synthetic dropouts.
  • Confirm & Fill: ~X% great-circle MAE reduction at ≤Y ms end-to-end latency.
